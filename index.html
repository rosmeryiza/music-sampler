Fleetwood Mac is a British-American rock band who formed in 1967. Click on the band to learn more about them!

The founders of the band were Peter Green, Mick Fleetwood and Jeremy Spencer. The band saw mainstream success when Stevie Nicks and Lindsey Buckingham joined in 1975. Their self-titled album released that year reached No. 1 in the U.S. Their next album Rumours, released in 1977, had four Top 10 singles in the U.S. The band remained stable until the late 1980s when several members left. They reunited briefly to play at President Bill Clinton's inauguration. In 1997 the band reunited fully again. Fleetwood Mac is still touring to this day.
